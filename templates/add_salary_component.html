Allowance Examples
  • Housing Allowance - Fixed amount or percentage
  • Transport Allowance - Usually fixed
  • Medical Allowance - Fixed amount
  • Overtime Pay - Usually percentage or calculated separately
Deduction Examples
  • Pension Contribution - Usually 8% of basic salary
  • NHF (National Housing Fund) - 2.5% of basic salary
  • NHIS (Health Insurance) - 5% of basic salary
  • Loan Repayments - Fixed amounts
  • Union Dues - Fixed amounts
Tax Considerations

Most allowances are taxable and included in PAYE calculations. Some deductions like pension contributions may be tax-exempt.
